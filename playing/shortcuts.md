### Common shortcuts (PC)

- C: Character profile
- H: Hunting Log
- P: Abilities & Traits
- J: Journal
- U: Duty List
- Shift+U: Party Finder
- I: Inventory
- Ctrl+I: Armoury chest
- O: Social (Party members, friends list)
- B: Gathering Log
- N: Crafting Log
- Y: Fishing Log
- M: Map
- R: Autorun
- Shift+F: Focus Target
